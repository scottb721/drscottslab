Saturn V Diorama Build

A custom Lego Saturn V diorama build featuring lighting, fog, audio, motion and automated effects.

Overview

This build brings the Saturn V to life as a display diorama, combining custom electronics, lighting effects, fog, sound, and motion. The system is driven by Arduino and integrates with Home Assistant and WLED to create an automated launch sequence experience.

Features

Lighting effects, fog generation, countdown audio, motorised elements, Home Assistant control, and WLED-driven LED sequences.
